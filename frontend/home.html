The Head Health application allows interactive interfacing between clinicians and their patients. Focusing primarily on mental health, the app allows a reciever of mental health support to review their daily progress, monitor therapy homework completion and review feedback from their assigned professionals.

Change Mental Health from Change Mental Health on Vimeo.

The technology

The product is delivered via an iOS app and a dynamic web site; using a database to manage information for users.

The Dashboard

This is the heart of Head Health, allowing quick and accurate feedback from patients on their current state of mind and mood. There is also access to feedback from practitioners and an overview of the data collected so far and the trends this reveals.
