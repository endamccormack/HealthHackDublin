The Head Health application allows interactive interfacing between clinicians and their patients. Focusing primarily on mental health, the app allows a reciever of mental health support to review their daily progress, monitor therapy homework completion and review feedback from their assigned professionals.

taggr-io from Click Innovate on Vimeo.

The technology

Near Field Communication

The Near Field Communication tags were procured from RapidNFC. They are a lightweight type that can hold 40 bytes, and can be used in place of stickers and wristbands which are commonly used in NHS wards for tracking patients and objects.
